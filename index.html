What Is the Mercedes Emissions Claim?

The Mercedes emissions claim centers around allegations that the company used illegal software-known as defeat devices-to manipulate emissions testing results. These devices made vehicles appear more environmentally friendly during tests while emitting significantly higher levels of nitrogen oxide (NOx) during real-world driving conditions.

Key Facts About the Case:

  • Affected Vehicles: Diesel models manufactured between 2008 and 2018.

  • Defeat Devices: Software designed to reduce emissions during testing but deactivate under normal driving conditions.

  • Legal Basis: Claims are based on consumer fraud and environmental damage caused by misleading emissions data.
